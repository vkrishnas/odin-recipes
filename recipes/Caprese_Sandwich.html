Caprese Sandwich

Ingredients
  • 2 slices of sourdough or ciabatta bread
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
    •  Instructions
    1. Toast the bread lightly.
    2. Layer tomato, mozzarella, and basil on one slice.
    3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, sprinkle salt and pepper.
    4. Top with the second slice of bread.
    5. Grill or press lightly for a warm sandwich (optional).
