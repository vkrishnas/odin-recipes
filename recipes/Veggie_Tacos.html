Ingredients

  • 4 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup black beans (cooked)
  • 1/2 avocado (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup corn (boiled or roasted)
  • Chopped onion and tomato
  • Lime wedges
  • Hot sauce or salsa
  • Fresh cilantro
Instructions
  1. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  2. Fill each with black beans, corn, tomato, onion, and avocado.
  3. Drizzle with lime juice and hot sauce.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.
