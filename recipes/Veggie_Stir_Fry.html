Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add all veggies, stir-fry on high heat for 5–6 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, mix well.
  5. Cook for 2 more minutes until veggies are tender-crisp.
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with rice or noodles.
